01 — About

A little about me

I'm the Co-Founder & CFO at Sonar AI — a startup using AI and computer vision to transform physical books into professional-quality audiobooks at a fraction of traditional production costs. We built our own OCR pipeline, text-to-speech synthesis, and book-scanning system from the ground up, and have won multiple recognitions for it. As CFO, I own financial modeling, pricing strategy, and business development.

I'm also an AI Engineer Apprentice at CMKL University, pursuing a Bachelor's in AI and Computer Engineering (QPA 3.8/4.0). Previously, I was a NOC Data Analyst intern at Agoda and most recently a Project Leader intern at True Alpha, leading a cross-functional team. My background spans startup, enterprise, and research environments.

Outside of tech, I work as an education consultant helping local students. I'm most alive where AI, business, and data converge — building financial models, designing ML pipelines, or thinking through product strategy.
